"Small But Mighty!"

Product: Sea Monkeys

My Sea Monkeys are doing well. They are low maintenance but you have to follow instructions just right. I am enjoying having them on my desk at work. They live, die, eat and mate right on my desk.
-A Guilbalt, Phoenix AZ

"What a terrible idea"

Product: Moon Shoes

Save your money...Because if you buy these for your kids you are going to need your money for the hospital....They do not work like you think that would..You kid is only bouncing on big rubber bands...And fall ever time they try to bounce...very dangerous..they aren't made for anyone..they should be taken off the market

"My son is obsessed!"

Product: Evel Knievel

I had one of these as a young boy and loved it. I saw this reissue and just had to buy one for my boy. We are having the absolute time of our lives, making jumps and doing stunts. This toy is great, super and fantastic, all rolled into a fiery loop of pure, spectacular joy. If you are a young boy or a middle aged man and don't have one, then you are missing out. Get one!!!
-Mikey Chook, Buford WY

"Actual Monkey??"

Product: Sea Monkeys

I genuinely thought I was getting a sweet little shrimp-like creature for my grandson. Came in the mail... little plastic bin and a small pouch full of rice-like junk. Pour it in the water. Wait 2 days... AND IT'S ACTUAL DAMN MONKEYS ALL UP IN MY HOUSE.
-Dolores Smith, Boca Raton FL

"Teddy bit my finger"

Product: Teddy Ruxpin

My uncle was so excited to finally see my sister's reaction when she ripped off the Happy Birthday wrapping paper. Unfortunately, that reaction was irrepressible panic. She screamed, she cried, she hid under a table. Eventually I inherited the thing, and we were pals while it was light outside. As soon as the sun went down though, you can bet Teddy was shackled to a table in the playroom.
-Patty Mayonnaise, Bloatsburg VA
